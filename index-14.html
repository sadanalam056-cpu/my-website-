Our Story

A taste of Araria's
warmest table

Tucked in the heart of Araria, Bihar, SBR Restaurant and Cafe brings together the comfort of home-style North Indian cooking, the fragrance of slow-cooked biryani, and the wok-fired energy of Chinese favourites — all served in a space built for good company.

Every dish leaves our kitchen the way it should: fresh spices ground in-house, biryani sealed and dum-cooked to order, and a menu that welcomes everyone from a quick evening chai to a full family celebration.
